Harriet Tubman

Harriet Tubman escaped slavery and become a leading abolitionist, helping lots of slaves escape to freedom.

Harriet also helped the Union Army during the war by working as a spy.

She then dedicated her life to helping slaves escape from slavery to freedom.

Harriet Tubman Wikipedia

  1. She was a conductor of the underground railroad.
  2. She guided at least 70 slaves to freedom.
  3. She was a Union scout and spy for during the American Civil War.
  4. She had an important role in the campaign for women's suffrage.
  5. She established The Harriet Tubman home for the Aged for "aged and indigent colored people".
